About us

Founded in 2023, OceanRiders is an international surf school offering a unique life experience where apprentices can live the daily life of surfers by staying in a surf house accompanied by their coach. Our coaches have the role of motivating you, advising you and offering you a unique experience in exceptional settings. OceanRiders' mission is to establish partnerships with professional surfers who are willing to pass on their knowledge and passion. We are proud to offer both personalized and individual lessons as well as group lessons to teach you how to surf and develop your skills in this exciting sport. Whether you are an absolute beginner or an experienced surfer looking to perfect your technique, our experienced coaches will be able to adapt to your level and guide you throughout your journey. With locations in France, Morocco and Australia, we offer you a unique opportunity to surf in dream destinations while benefiting from the advice of our experts. Join us for an unforgettable experience and discover the magnificent spots that our passionate instructors have in store for you.
